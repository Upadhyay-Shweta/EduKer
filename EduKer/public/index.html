Who We Are

Government of Kerla

As the Government of Kerala, our profound commitment lies in ensuring the holistic development of every child, particularly in rural areas. With a visionary approach, we aim to provide maximum resources to children in these regions, recognizing that education is a cornerstone for their future success. Through strategic initiatives, we focus on building robust educational infrastructure, allocating financial resources to alleviate economic constraints, and implementing awareness programs to empower communities with the significance of education. Our goal is to create a conducive environment that nurtures the intellectual, social, and personal growth of every child, fostering a generation equipped to overcome socio-economic challenges.
